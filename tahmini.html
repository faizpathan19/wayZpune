Welcome to Tamhini Ghat

Nestled in the estern Ghats of Maharashtra, Tamhini Ghat is a scenic mountain pass known for its lush green valleys, mesmerizing waterfalls, and misty atmosphere. It is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers.



Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit is from June to October.
- Monsoon (June - September): Ideal for enjoying waterfalls and dense greenery.
- Post-Monsoon (October - November): Pleasant weather with clear views of the valleys.



Must-Visit Places

  • Mulshi Lake: A serene lake perfect for relaxation and photography.
  • Devkund Waterfall: A hidden gem with a stunning plunge waterfall.
  • Tamhini Waterfalls: Series of breathtaking waterfalls best enjoyed during monsoon.
