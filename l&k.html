Welcome to Lonavala & Khandala

Nestled in the **Western Ghats of Maharashtra**, Lonavala and Khandala are two of the most beloved hill stations near **Mumbai and Pune**. Famous for their **lush green valleys, misty hills, and beautiful waterfalls**, these twin destinations offer the perfect weekend getaway for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and food enthusiasts.



Best Time to Visit


The best time to visit Lonavala and Khandala is from June to February.
- Monsoon (June - September): Ideal for scenic beauty, waterfalls, and fog-covered mountains.
- (October - February): Perfect for trekking and enjoying cool weather.
- (March - May): Warmer but still pleasant compared to cities.



Must-Visit Places


  • Bhushi Dam: A famous monsoon destination where visitors can enjoy splashing in overflowing water.
  • Lohagad Fort: A historic fort offering breathtaking views and a great trekking experience.
  • Karla & Bhaja Caves: Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves with intricate carvings and history dating back to the 2nd century BC.
