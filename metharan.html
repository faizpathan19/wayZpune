Welcome to Matheran

Matheran, located in the Western Ghats of Maharashtra, is India‚Äôs only eco-sensitive hill station where no motor vehicles are allowed. With lush greenery, mist-covered valleys, and breathtaking viewpoints, it is the perfect escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers.



Best Time to Visit


The best time to visit Matheran is from October to May. - Monsoon (June - September): Offers stunning misty views but trails can get slippery. - Winter (October - February): The best season for trekking, sightseeing, and exploring. - Summer (March - May): Pleasant weather, perfect for a weekend getaway.



Must-Visit Places


  • Panorama Point: Famous for its 360-degree views of the Sahyadri mountains and sunrise views.
  • Louisa Point: A perfect spot for enjoying breathtaking valley views and waterfalls during monsoon.
  • Echo Point: Known for its natural echo phenomenon, a fun place for shouting and hearing your voice bounce back.
  • Charlotte Lake: A peaceful lake surrounded by dense forests, perfect for relaxation.
  • One Tree Hill Point: A unique viewpoint with a lone tree standing on a hill, offering mesmerizing views.


Things to Do in Matheran


Matheran is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers: - Trekking: Enjoy scenic trails leading to viewpoints like One Tree Hill and Louisa Point. - Toy Train Ride: Experience the famous Matheran Toy Train, offering a slow but scenic ride through forests. - Horse Riding: Since no cars are allowed, horses are the main mode of transport. - Nature Walks: Walk through the lush green forests and enjoy the peaceful environment. - Shopping: Buy local chikki, leather goods, and handicrafts from the Matheran market.
