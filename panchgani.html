Welcome to Panchgani

Located in the Sahyadri Hills of Maharashtra, Panchgani is a serene hill station known for its breathtaking viewpoints, strawberry farms, and colonial-era charm. It is an ideal destination for a peaceful retreat.



Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit is from October to May.
- Monsoon (June - September): Perfect for lush green landscapes and waterfalls.
- Winter (October - February): Best for sightseeing and outdoor adventures.
- Summer (March - May): Enjoy the cool climate and fresh strawberries.



Must-Visit Places

  • Table Land: Asia’s second-largest mountain plateau with stunning views.
  • Parsi Point: A scenic viewpoint overlooking the Krishna Valley.
  • Devil’s Kitchen: A historical cave linked to the Mahabharata.


Where to Eat & Stay

🍽️ Best Restaurants

  • Lucky Restaurant: Known for delicious North Indian cuisine.
  • Hilltop Ice Cream: Famous for homemade ice creams and desserts.


🏨 Best Hotels & Resorts

  • Ravine Hotel: A luxurious stay with modern amenities.
  • Il Palazzo Hotel: A heritage hotel with an old-world charm.
