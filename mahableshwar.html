Welcome to Mahabaleshwar

Nestled in the Sahyadri Hills of Maharashtra, Mahabaleshwar is known for its stunning viewpoints, lush greenery, and pleasant climate. It is perfect for a relaxing getaway and adventure activities.



Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit is from October to May.
- Monsoon (June - September): Ideal for nature lovers with lush greenery and waterfalls.
- Winter (October - February): Best for sightseeing and outdoor activities.
- Summer (March - May): Great escape from the heat with cool breezes.



Must-Visit Places

  • Arthur’s Seat: A famous viewpoint offering mesmerizing valley views.
  • Venna Lake: A serene spot for boating and relaxing.
  • Lingmala Waterfall: A breathtaking waterfall, especially beautiful during monsoon.


Where to Eat & Stay

🍽️ Best Restaurants

  • Bagicha Corner: Famous for strawberry cream and local snacks.
  • Mapro Garden Café: Best place for fresh fruit-based dishes.


🏨 Best Hotels & Resorts

  • Evershine Resort: A luxurious stay with modern amenities.
  • Brightland Resort: Offers stunning valley views and great hospitality.
